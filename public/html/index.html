About Us

We are a platform for local communities to post events, raise money for charitable causes, and a map feature to optimize recycling efforts.

Mapping System

Based on your location, you will receive the locations of the nearest recycling centers and also the optimal locations for new recycling spots based on our optimization algorithm. This helps small communities to scale their impact with limited resources.

Additional features include optimal mappings between recycling centers to take shortest paths to span the most area.

  • Posting Board for local volunteering/recycling events
  • Donation System to a distributed asset class of charities and non-profit organizations.

In addition to this, we have videos promoting the importance of recycling. The leaderboard for donations is to recognize top contributors and inspire others to donate to a good cause.
